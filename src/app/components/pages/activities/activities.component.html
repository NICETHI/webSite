1. Tandem Pairs: We would map 2 people who could improve their language knowledge by interchangeably sharing.
  2. Tandem Families: We would map a group of 4-6 people of the same language level. Our motivation to do this activity is that students could help each other with the techniques that they found useful while they are practising the language and to build a small community around language.
  3. Tandem Meets: We are going to continue with an online format for at least the first part of the semester. We are determined to do the meetings in real life again as soon as possible, of course when the situation gets better and also if you are interested!
  4. Tandem Acts: We want to start this activity the next semester. In this activity, we would group all the students who are learning a particular language. The students from this group could make a team of 4-6 people and write a script and/or make a short film of up to 4-8 minutes on the topic that you like...but the specific details would be shared with you after you enrol. There would be a contest and the winners would get a certificate and a worthy prize.
